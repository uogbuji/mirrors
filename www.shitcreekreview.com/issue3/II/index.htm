While the Shit Creek Review generally has no special theme per issue, each II is likely to be slanted in a specific direction or have content relating to a single common theme. This inaugural issue looks at the work of Timothy Murphy, poet-farmer of the American Upper Midwest.
If you’d like to contribute text or art to a future II, please see the submission guidelines in SCR.
