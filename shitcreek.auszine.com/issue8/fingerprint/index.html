Issue 8 — Obsession and Compulsion

Fingerprint

by Robin Helweg-Larsen

I want to fingerprint you
All over.
I want to fingerpaint you
All over.
I want to finger you, finger you,
Linger on and lingua you,
Finger you everywhere,
Everyhow, everywho,
Finger you, all of you,
Paint and imprint over you,
All of all
All over.



art by Patricia Wallace Jones
