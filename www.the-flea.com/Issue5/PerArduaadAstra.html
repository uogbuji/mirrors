Per Ardua ad Astra

by Geoff Page

Just the sadder
side of dumpy
she’s working in this 
transfat place,
pulling halfway 
decent coffees.
There is no future 
in her face. 

Cappuccinos 
take a while;
your mind’s relaxing
in the car.
With just a few twists 
of a spoon
she hands you an 
exquisite star.
Recent books by Geoff page include Seriatim (Salt) http://www.saltpublishing.com/books/smp/9781844713004.htm and 60 Classic Australian Poems (UNSW Press) http://www.unswpress.com.au/isbn/9781921410796.htm

February, 2010
Geoff Page is an Australian poet who has published eighteen collections of poetry as well as two novels, four verse novels and several other works including anthologies, translations and a biography of the jazz musician, Bernie McGann. He has won several awards, including the ACT Poetry Award, the Grace Leven Prize, the Christopher Brennan Award, the Queensland Premier’s Prize for Poetry and the 2001 Patrick White Literary Award.
