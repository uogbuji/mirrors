June Bugs

by Timothy Murphy

June bugs careen
night after night
against my screen.

In their desire
for warmth and light
they too expire.

First published in Timothy Murphy’s The Deed of Gift, Story Line Press
8
May, 2010
Timothy Murphy hunts in the Dakotas.
